# Pixel Pals

## About the Game

Pixel Pals is a 2D platformer where every level is a rescue mission. Your friends have been scattered across dangerous, zombie-infested worlds — and it's up to you to reach them.

Each level places your friends waiting at the far end of the map. To get to them, you'll need to fight through waves of zombies, dodge deadly traps, and navigate increasingly challenging platforming sections. The closer you get to your pals, the harder the world pushes back.

---

## Gameplay

- **Rescue your friends** — Each level ends when you reach your friends waiting on the other side of the map
- **Battle zombies** — Enemies stand between you and your goal; fight or find a way around them
- **Dodge traps** — Environmental hazards are scattered throughout each level, punishing the careless
- **Platform your way through** — Navigate the terrain using your movement and reflexes to survive the journey

---

## Features

- 2D side-scrolling platformer built in **Godot**
- Level-based progression with increasing difficulty
- A mix of combat and skill-based platforming
- Friends to rescue at the end of each stage

---

## Goal

Pixel Pals is about more than just surviving — it's about making sure no one gets left behind. Every zombie you take down and every trap you dodge brings you one step closer to your friends. Clear every level, rescue every pal.
